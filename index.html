About Us

Originating in early 2017, The Stratus Network aims to be the next home for the PGM (PVP Game Manager) plugin which was originally made for the now closed Overcast Network. With PGM and other custom coded plugins, Stratus offers competitive and casual team-based pvp and most importantly, a dedicated and awesome community.
